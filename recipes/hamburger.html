Hamburger

Description

I love hamburgers, but hate cooking them. I'm so bad at cleaning up after cooking.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger (Meat)
  • Cheese (Optional)
  • Bun (Optional)
  • Hotel (Trivago)

Steps

  1. Mold hamburger meat into patty
  2. Cook on medium heat for 15 minutes, flipping
  3. Add cheese
  4. Place on bun and enjoy
