Coffee

Description

I drink this every day. I'm drinking it right now. If you ever stop by [local coffee shop], get 'Brazil', it's by far the best.

Ingredients

  • Coffee Beans
  • Water
  • Milk or Creamer

Steps

  1. Grind beans medium fine
  2. Place in coffee maker with water
  3. Start coffee maker and wait until fully brewed
  4. Pour into a mug and add creamer to taste
